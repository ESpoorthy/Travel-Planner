## 🚗 Getting Around

**Best Options:**
1. **Uber/Ola:** Most convenient, ₹200-500 per ride
2. **Auto Rickshaws:** Cheaper, use meter or negotiate
3. **Metro:** Connects major areas, ₹10-60 per ride
4. **TSRTC Buses:** Cheapest, ₹10-30 per ride
5. **Rental Car:** ₹2,000-3,000 per day with driver

## 🎯 Pro Tips

1. **Best Time to Visit:** October-March (pleasant weather)
2. **Avoid:** May-June (very hot, 40-45°C)
3. **Language:** Telugu, Hindi, English widely spoken
4. **Dress Code:** Modest for religious places
5. **Bargaining:** Expected in markets, not in malls
6. **Spice Level:** Hyderabadi food is spicy, ask for mild
7. **Water:** Drink bottled water
8. **Safety:** Generally safe, avoid isolated areas at night
